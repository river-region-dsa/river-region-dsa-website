Our Work

The River Region Democratic Socialists of America are dedicated to building a more just and equitable world through collective action, and we believe that starts at home, in our local community. We stand in solidarity with workers who never see their fair share of the wealth that their labor generates. We advocate for humane working conditions, and the right to organize and be represented within the workplace. Our commitment to mutual aid means providing direct support to our communities, ensuring that no one is left behind in times of need, especially at a time when our institutions are under constant assault.

We believe in the power of intersectionality — recognizing that struggles for justice are deeply connected. We fight for racial justice, LGBTQ+ rights, and the liberation of all marginalized communities here and abroad. By fighting for inclusivity, and standing against all forms of discrimination and oppression, we work together to build a community where everyone can succeed and thrive.

Democracy is at the heart of our movement, and as such we believe it is necessary to fight for the expansion of voting rights and oppose any form of coercion—whether in the workplace, the legal system, or in our streets. By our deeds and our activism, we strive to create a future rooted in fairness, dignity, and true democracy for all.
